# AI Voice Agent Pricing Research - Australian Market (April 2026)

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors (Tradie-focused AI receptionists in Australia)

**LANA Software**
- AI Receptionist only: **$300/month** + $1,000 setup
  - Handles inbound calls
  - Books appointments automatically
  - Sends call recordings
- AI + Job Creation: **$399/month** + $1,500 setup
  - Qualifies calls
  - Creates jobs in JMS (Simpro, ServiceM8, Ascora, etc.)
- Full CRM + AI: **$600/month** + $3,500 setup
  - Multi-channel (phone, SMS, social, website)
  - Auto quote/invoice chasing
  - Google review requests

**Johnni.ai**
- Pricing: **$300-$1,000+/month** (exact tiers not publicly listed)
- Positioning: "Best AI receptionist in Australia" (premium positioning)
- Features: 24/7, real-time booking, custom workflows, Aussie voice
- Claims: Most clients recoup investment in first month

**Sophiie AI**
- **$300/month** + $800 setup ($400 with discount)
- Flat monthly pricing
- 10,000 simultaneous calls
- Australian accent, 24/7

### Broader Market (General AI answering services)

**Budget tier:**
- $48-$150/month (basic bots, limited features)
- Often sound robotic, poor handling of complex queries

**Mid-market:**
- $200-$500/month (most common range)
- Includes call answering, basic booking, CRM sync

**Premium:**
- $600-$1,400+/month
- Multi-channel, advanced integrations, white-label options

**Enterprise:**
- $2,500-$10,000+/month (managed services)
- Custom builds, dedicated support

## Key Insights

1. **Setup fees are standard**: $400-$3,500 depending on complexity
2. **$300-$400/month is the competitive sweet spot** for tradie-focused AI receptionists
3. **Premium positioning works**: Johnni.ai charges up to $1,000+/month by emphasizing quality over cost
4. **Call volume limits vary**: Some charge per-minute overages, others offer unlimited
5. **14-day free trials are common** in this market
6. **ROI messaging is critical**: "How much are missed calls costing you?" resonates

### Why This Works

1. **$247 undercuts LANA/Sophiie** ($300) to capture price-sensitive solo tradies
2. **$497 sits between LANA's $399 and $600 tiers** — premium features without full CRM complexity
3. **No setup fees** (differentiator) — absorb training costs into monthly to reduce barrier
4. **Call volume limits are generous** but not unlimited (protects margin)
5. **14-day trial matches market standard**
6. **Professional tier is highlighted** to anchor mid-market buyers

### Margin Notes

- Average cost per minute for AI voice: $0.05-$0.15
- 500 calls/month @ 3 min average = 1,500 minutes = $75-$225 COGS
- $497/month leaves healthy margin for support, infrastructure, and growth
